# tiempo.fitness — Design System

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Tiempo Fitness is a fitness app landing page built in Framer that commits fully to darkness and electric energy. The near-black canvas (`rgb(10,10,10)`) is the deepest of dark modes, and against it, a single explosive accent detonates: electric yellow-green (`rgb(225,255,0)`) — almost neon, almost chartreuse. The heading palette uses a warm cream (`rgb(242,231,223)`) that feels organic and body-conscious against the darkness. Three typefaces handle three distinct registers: Apris for powerful modern headings, SF Compact Rounded for stats/numbers, and Tiempos Text for editorial body copy.

## 3. Typography Rules

A three-family system, each carving out its own register:

- `h2`: **Apris Medium**, 40px, weight 400, −0.8px tracking — clean modern fitness
- `h3`: **Apris Medium**, 32px, weight 400, −0.64px tracking
- `h5`: **SF Compact Rounded Medium**, 34px — rounded numerics, stats display
- `h6`: **Input Sans Regular**, 11px, uppercase, +1.65px tracking — category labels/tags
- `p`: **Tiempos Text Regular**, 16px, −0.32px tracking — editorial body copy
- `body`: system sans-serif at 12px (Framer default)

Apris provides the bold fitness identity. SF Compact Rounded handles the sporty number display. Tiempos Text brings editorial credibility to body content. Input Sans uppercase labels with wide tracking create a technical, app-like tag system.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Three dark surfaces (10,10,10 → 18,18,18 → 23,23,23) create depth through luminance alone. No shadows. The electric yellow appears at luminance 0.851–0.860 — maximum contrast against the deepest darks.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

**Do:** Commit to the near-black canvas. Use electric yellow exclusively for the primary CTA or hero accent — one clear signal. Maintain the three-typeface hierarchy: Apris headings, SF Compact stats, Tiempos body.

**Don't:** Add warm background colors — cream exists only in text. Don't dilute the electric yellow with alternatives. Don't round corners — the sharp edges complement the dark, technical aesthetic.
