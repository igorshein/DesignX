## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

The Social Brain is a marketing or growth agency that channels energy through red. The design is clean and white-dominant, but a vivid coral-red (`#f15757`) punches through at key moments тАФ a brand element that reads equally as confident urgency and approachable warmth. ## 3. Typography Rules

A two-family system:

- `h1`: `heading` (custom), 48px, weight 700, line-height 80px тАФ generous leading
- `h2`: `heading`, 24px, weight 700
- `h3`: `heading`, 30px, weight 700
- `p`: `heading`, 24px, weight 700 тАФ paragraphs use the heading font at heading weight (unusual, suggests large body-as-display treatment)
- `h4/body/button/input`: Geist, 16тАУ20px, weight 400
- `label`: system sans-serif, 14px, weight 500

The `p` using the heading font at 700 weight suggests editorial-style large body text rather than conventional body copy.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Shadow system: outer drop `rgba(88,88,88,0.25) 0px 4px 4px` combined with inner inset `rgba(162,162,162,0.25) 3px 6px 3px inset`. This creates a slightly dimensional, plastic-like quality for button/card elements.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

**Do:** Use the heading font at bold weights for all display text including large body paragraphs. Use Geist for functional UI elements. Apply coral red sparingly тАФ it's the brand accent, not a background.

**Don't:** Use light font weights тАФ this design leans bold. Don't add multiple accent colors beyond the red variants. Don't suppress the inset shadow on CTAs.
