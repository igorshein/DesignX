## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

eu-inc.org carries the aesthetic authority of institutional minimalism. Built in Framer, the design uses a cool light-gray canvas (`rgb(245,245,245)`) as a neutral institutional ground — neither warm nor cold, simply present. The typographic system pairs **Inter Display** for headings with **Apfel Grotezk** for body copy, a combination that balances Swiss rationalism with the warm personality Apfel brings.

The site signals credibility through restraint: limited color, compact type scale, and clean negative space. It feels like a thoughtfully designed annual report or policy brief made interactive.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Use the three-step gray scale for surface layering
- Keep type compact — 12–16px range, no display-scale headlines
- Use Inter Display at 600 weight with negative tracking for headings
- Use Apfel Grotezk for body paragraphs — its humanist character softens the dense layout
- Treat link color as a semantic signal, not a brand element

**Don't:**
- Add brand colors or accent hues — institutional neutrality is the identity
- Use rounded corners — sharp edges reinforce the formal tone
- Add decorative animation — Framer Motion transitions should be minimal
- Scale up typography for marketing effect
