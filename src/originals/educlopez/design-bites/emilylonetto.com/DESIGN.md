## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Emily Lonetto's portfolio is a masterclass in typographic maximalism with a rigorously playful edge. **Cooperhewitt** — a geometric humanist sans — is set at monstrous scale (181px+ for H1), uppercase, with aggressive negative tracking (−12.7px). Two accent colors — a vivid yellow (`#FFF25D`) and electric blue (`#146EF5`) — punctuate the predominantly neutral field, used for eyebrow labels and highlights. GSAP powers the animation layer, suggesting intentional motion choreography.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Set display type in Cooperhewitt uppercase with strong negative tracking
- Use yellow and blue accents sparingly, only for short labels/highlights
- Pair bold display type with IBM Plex Mono for contrast
- Use GSAP for entrance animations — staggered, purposeful
- Maintain generous section padding (90px+)

**Don't:**
- Mix more than three typefaces
- Use color for backgrounds — keep canvas white
- Add border-radius; corners stay sharp
- Use subtle animations; if something moves, it's intentional and visible
